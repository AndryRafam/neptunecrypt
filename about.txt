 NeptuneCrypt is a free open-source encryption software using
 XChaCha20Poly1305 as cipher for single encryption and XChaCha-Aes
 as cascade cipher for cascade encryption.
 
 Password is generated randomly.
 You can choose the password length between 16 to 256 characters.
